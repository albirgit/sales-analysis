## 📌 Project Overview

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Top Selling Category (by Quantity)
- **Furniture** emerged as the top-selling category by quantity.
- This indicates high customer demand, possibly due to frequent small-ticket purchases or bulk orders.

### 2️⃣ Highest Selling Category (by Sales Value)
- **Home Decor** generated the highest total revenue.
- Although it may sell fewer units than Furniture, its **higher price per item** drives more revenue.

### 3️⃣ Highest Selling Month
- **May 2025** recorded the highest sales.
- This spike may be linked to **seasonal demand**, promotions, or special sales campaigns.

### 4️⃣ City with Highest Profit
- **Bangalore** contributed the highest overall profit.
- This suggests strong purchasing power and a mature e-commerce market in the region.
